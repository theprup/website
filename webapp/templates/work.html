Work

Here's some highlight's from my career to date...

Plum

At Plum I was the Product Manager responsible for the Plum's subscription offering (Plum Plus and Plum Pro). When I started we had 10-15% of new customers subscribing. During my time at Plum we increased this to 25-30% of new users.

Some cool features I worked on

  • Diagnostics: delivers a monthly report that contextualises spending by looking at user habits by category and benchmarks spending against other users with a similar financial profile (based on factors such as salary, location and age)
  • True Balance: takes some of the guess-work out of budgeting for users by giving them a rolling account balance which has been adjusted to provide a true financial picture (i.e. once any bills or other forecasted payments have also been considered)
  • Pay Days: lets as user specify when they get paid, and suggests and amount (5-10% of users salary) to automatically deposit to their Plum account
  • Interest accounts: users can receive 0.6% interest on their Plum savings
  • Faster payments: enabled faster payments service (via Open Banking) so users can deposit instantly into their Plum account

Spaceship

At Spaceship I was a Product Manager and then the Head of Product. During my time at Spaceship I helped with the development and launch of Voyager, Spaceship's full-stack investment app. When I left Spaceship it had 50,000 customers and $40m under management.
