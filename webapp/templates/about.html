I live in London and am currently on sabbatical as I try and figure out what I want to do next. I grew up in New Zealand and studied Law & English at Victoria University. From there I joined Uber in Melbourne where I was one of the early employees trying to scale UberX in an unregulated and sometimes hostile rideshare market. From there I joined Spaceship as employee ~6 where I helped grow the business to over 45,000 customers and $200m in AUM, when I left in 2019. Most recently I was a Product Manager at Plum where I was responsible for the subscription products (Plum Pro & Plum Plus).
